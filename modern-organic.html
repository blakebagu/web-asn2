ORGANIC MODERN

Often paring elements of nature against industrial architecture, Organic Modern focuses on bringing nature inside the home. in line with 2023's environmental trends, this style often includes copius amounts of plants and other organic materials (such as wicker and clay) inside harsh and simple interiors. it's this juxdaposition that makes Organic Modern so popular, as it makes it easily attainable and instantly recognizable. In addition. it is often very minimal, to emphasize its small footprint and encourage others to live more simply.
