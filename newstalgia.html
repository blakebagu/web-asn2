NEWSTALGIA

Mixing nostalgic elements with a contemporary aesthetic, 'Newstalgia' took homes by storm in 2023. it often incorporates vintage or thrifted statement pieces nestled in a modern setting, in line with this years' focus on re-using and recycling. you'll often find antique pieces of decor such as Grandfather clocks, old records, or classical oil paintings in these rooms, juxdaposed with modern furnishings.
