COLOUR WASHING

Colour washing took the world (and its walls) by storm in 2023. Colour washing is a technique in which the same colour (or a very similar colour) of paint is applied to the walls, trim, and possibly ceiling of a room, 'washing' it in that colour. This trend is often followed by using decor and furniture that is a similar hue to the walls, to create rooms that are essentially one colour. This speaks to 2023's focus on minimalism, though this trend is not neccessarily minimal in its decor, it is minimal in its use of colour, and that counts too.
