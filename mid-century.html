MID-CENTURY MODERN

Emerging in the mid 20th century, mid-century modern is characterized by its use of natural materials, geometric shapes, and large open spaces that blend indoor and outdoor. With 2023's heavy focus on environmental design, its no surprise that mid-century modern has become so popular, given that many of its spaces emphasize wood and stone furnishings, and it's retro aesthetic often uses antique and thrifted pieces.
